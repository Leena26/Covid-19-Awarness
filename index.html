HOW TO STAY SAFE FROM COVID-19

~Made by Prograd Jr, Leena

Wash Your Hands Regularly

Wash you hands regularly with soap or
sanitizer for 30 seconds.
This will help prevent germs.

Cover Your Mouth and Nose

Cover your mouth and nose while coughing
or sneezing in public. The best thing would
be to wear a mask.

Stay Home Stay Safe

Avoid travelling or being in
crowded places. Prefer staying indoors.

Eat Helathy

Avoid consuming junk food. Eat as
healthy as possible to boost your immunity.

Stay Calm

Regular exercise or meditation is a
great way to keep your self calm
and composed.

BE AWARE. BE SAFE.
